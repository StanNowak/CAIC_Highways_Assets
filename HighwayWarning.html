Overall Corridor Avalanche Hazard Rating

The next storm will impact the corridor Tuesday through Thursday. Expect the avalanche hazard to rise Tuesday night and mitigation on Wednesday or Thursday morning.

Operational Recommendations

Please check with a CAIC Forecaster before doing any work under the Seven Sisters group. Travel through this area is okay. Maintenance work in other parts of Loveland Pass is okay.

Mitigation Recommendations

Expect hazard mitigation in the Seven Sisters group on Wednesday or Thursday morning. We don't expect to do mitigation in other paths on Loveland Pass.
